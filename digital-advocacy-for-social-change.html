Digital Advocacy for Social Change

Developing and Distributing content to inspire social change

If you work in advocacy you probably know the feeling of struggling to get your message out there. Our digital advocacy programme can help.

We work on individual advocacy issues, to develop and distribute content, with a view to nudging people's behaviours and structural beliefs, or informing the public on relevant issues. We can tackle any progressive advocacy concern – from the structural (racism, sexism etc) to the information led (extremism, refugees, education, health etc) to the particular (Boko Haram, Brexit etc).

In order to produce content, we use Opinion – our social media research analytics tool powered by Crimson Hexagon. We isolate an issue, produce 5-6 tailored research pieces on it and then use our social media network to distribute.

We are convinced that our Digital Advocacy work provides the best possible way to analyse and reflect citizen’s perceptions, nudge or inform citizens about pertinent causes and provide unparalleled distribution of pertinent, original material on almost any progressive cause.

We have run a case study on Boko Haram at our own cost to prove our case. A full study might look at – the rise of fear, perceptions around funding, perceptions around why people join, the main drivers of radicalisation, personal stories and so on. We focused on two of these, the rise of fear and perceptions around how Boko Haram is funded.
