Funding

Partner with us

We are funded by Vibrand Research and by our bespoke research work. We are eager to expand our offering – especially reporting into the public domain – and are open to partnership/funding from socially active organisations. Please contact us if you wish to discuss funding our work.

Partnership Possibilities

We are in the process of forming partnerships and developing networks of funders.

We are open to both per-project funding or funding for a particular piece of research.

We have partnered on individual projects with organisations such as the Institute for Security Studies, the Centre of Criminology at UCT, FCA, KAICIID, The Peace Network and the Global Institute for Transnational Crime. The Citizen Research Centre is an agile, flexible organisation. We can develop partnerships, projects or alliances in innovative ways.
